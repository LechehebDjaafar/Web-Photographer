About Me

El-Hadjra

El-Hadjra is a historic site located in Souk El-Hajar, in the city of Ouargla, Algeria. Renowned for its architectural beauty and cultural significance, this landmark stands as a testament to the region's rich heritage and authenticity. El-Hadjra serves as a vibrant hub, blending tradition with daily life, making it a favorite destination for both visitors and locals alike.

Ouargla Central Bus Station

The Ouargla Central Bus Station is a key transportation hub in the city of Ouargla, Algeria. Serving as the main gateway for travelers, it connects the entire region to various destinations across the country. Known for its bustling activity, the station is a vital part of daily life in Ouargla, providing a convenient and efficient travel experience for residents and visitors alike.

Kasdi Merbah University

Kasdi Merbah University, located in Ouargla, Algeria, is a prestigious institution known for its academic excellence and diverse programs. It serves as a cornerstone for higher education in the region, attracting students from across the country. The university is celebrated for fostering innovation, research, and development, contributing significantly to the intellectual and cultural growth of the area.
